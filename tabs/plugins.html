Core

minimal set of plugins needed to run the virtualop

Installation

Setup new machines from scratch

Deployment

Deploy pre-packaged services and your own projects from a variety of sources

Monitoring

With our monitoring system, you can detect downtimes and problems with the availability and scalability of your application.

Backup & Restore

In the metadata of your application, you specify what data you want to include in the backup, which is performed once a day. This backups not only become handy in the case of disc failures (>restore), but also provide useful information for your development and staging environments.

Database setup and replication

These plugins help you to setup (MySQL) databases.

Logfile collection & analysis

Be able to identify the problems and bugs that are lowering the functionality of your application without digging through tons of logfiles.

Continuous Integration & deployment

Deploying small changes can be a very complicated thing. With the Virtual Op, you can simply update them on your machines and...they're are already part of you application.

Automated testing

Before your changes are visible for the users of your application, you might want to make sure that they work first. With our testing system, you can check several parameters, like load, scalability, and availabilty.
